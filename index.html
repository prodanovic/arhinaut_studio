About

Studio Arhinaut was established in 2004, and since then it engaged a number of architects, designers and 3D artists to collaborate on exciting projects in the field of architecture, engineering and design.

The wide range of successfully completed projects has taught us to be prepared for anything and to always deliver beyond our clients' expectations, be it Al Zubarah museum in the desert, the naturalist museum in Belgrade or the design of the first Arabic Lego set for the Queen Latifa.
