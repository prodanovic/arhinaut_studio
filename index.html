ABOUT
US:

Arhinaut, a company based in Belgrade, Serbia, is engaged in all aspects architecture, engineering, design and consulting services by combining it contemporary methods of modern architecture.

Unconventional ideas along with various forms of architectural and construction solutions is what makes Arhinaut recognizable and unique.

The company covers a wide range of civil and urban design projects with their main team in Belgrade as well as a wide network of companies, associates and consultants of different profiles in the country and abroad.
